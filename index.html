About Me

"Giving a Voice to the Silent: Transforming Data into Lifelines."

In the world of prey animals, silence is a survival instinct. Rabbits rarely show pain until it's too late. I developed a specialized health screening application that translates a rabbit's most vital health indicator—their digestive output—into actionable insights.

I believe that technology's greatest purpose is to protect those who cannot speak for themselves. By turning daily observations into biometric data, my work empowers owners to detect early warning signs of GI Stasis and other critical conditions, ensuring that no bunny suffers in silence.

Featured Project

Rabbit Health Monitoring

AI-powered health tracking for rabbit owners

An innovative Flutter application that uses machine learning to analyze rabbit health through automated feces detection and classification. The app combines computer vision (YOLOv8), real-time database synchronization, and intelligent health recommendations.

Key Features:

  • 📷 AI-powered feces analysis with YOLOv8 TensorFlow Lite
  • 🐰 Manage and track multiple rabbit profiles
  • 📊 Health history with interactive charts
  • 🔄 Offline-first architecture with SQLite
  • ⚡ Real-time health status & recommendations
  • 📱 Cross-platform (Android & iOS)
Flutter Dart TensorFlow Lite SQLite BLoC Firebase
